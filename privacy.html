Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Plushify Me ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Plushify Me mobile application.

1) Information We Collect

Account information

  • Email address and basic account identifiers when you sign in with email, Google, or Apple.
  • Basic profile information provided by your sign-in provider (for example, display name and profile photo URL, if available).

Images you choose to process

  • Photos you select or capture in the app to generate plushie images.
  • Generated plushie output images created from your input.

Usage and app data

  • Weekly generation usage counters linked to your account (for limit and subscription logic).
  • Basic technical information needed to provide the service (such as request failures and connectivity events).

2) How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your account and keep you signed in.
  • Process your photos and generate plushie images.
  • Enforce free/subscription generation limits.
  • Save and display your generated images within the app.
  • Provide support and improve app reliability.

3) Third-Party Services

We use third-party providers to deliver core features:

  • Firebase Authentication for sign-in and account authentication.
  • Cloud Firestore to store account-linked usage counters.
  • OpenAI API to transform your selected image into plushie-style output.

Your selected image and prompt data are sent securely to the image-generation provider to return results. We recommend avoiding sensitive personal photos if you do not want them processed by third-party AI services.

4) Photos and Device Permissions

  • Camera: Used only when you choose to take a photo.
  • Photo Library: Used to select photos and optionally save generated images back to your device gallery.

We do not access your camera roll or camera unless you grant permission and trigger the action.

5) Data Retention

  • Generated images saved in-app remain until you delete them.
  • Account and usage-related records may remain while your account is active and as needed for service and legal compliance.

6) Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers needed to operate the app (authentication, storage, and image generation), and only for those purposes.

7) Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

8) Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent.

9) Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can remove locally saved generated images from within the app or your device.
  • You can contact us to request account-related data actions, subject to legal requirements.

10) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date on this page.

11) Contact

For privacy questions, contact: support@plushifyme.app
