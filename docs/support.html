Quick FAQ

Why did generation fail?

Check internet connectivity and try again. Temporary server timeouts can happen.

How do I save my plushie to Photos?

Tap the save icon on the result card and allow Photos permission when prompted.

Can I delete saved plushies?

Yes. You can remove generated images from the app gallery and from your photo library.

How do I reset my password?

Use the password reset flow on the login screen with your registered email.
